Matcha (Hojicha) Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies

Description:

This recipe is taken from this website as inspiration for first my odin project where I decide to use hojicha powder which is also green tea but has less caffeine. This recipe has not been tested however it is similar and only changes one ingredient so depending on the concentration/flavor intensity it should provide similar results.

Ingredients:

  • 110g Unsalted Butter
  • 100g Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 90g All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Hojicha powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt (Omit if using salted butter)
  • 130g Rolled Oats
  • 100g Chocolate Chips
